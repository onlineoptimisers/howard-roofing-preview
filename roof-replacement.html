Why Replace Your Roof

Protect What Matters Most

A damaged or aging roof puts your entire home at risk. Water intrusion, mold, structural damage, and energy loss all start with a roof past its prime. In coastal North Carolina, the stakes are even higher.

  • Storm and hurricane damage that compromises structural integrity
  • Aging shingles past their 20-25 year lifespan
  • Persistent leaks that patching cannot fix
  • Rising energy bills from poor insulation and ventilation
  • Curb appeal and home value for resale

Built for the Coast

Why Wilmington Roofs Need Special Attention

Coastal North Carolina is one of the toughest environments for a roof. Between hurricanes, salt air, UV exposure, and heavy rain, the materials and techniques matter more here than almost anywhere else in the country.

That is why we do not just install roofs. We engineer them for this specific climate. Every project accounts for:

  • Hurricane-force wind resistance (130+ MPH rated materials)
  • Salt air corrosion protection on fasteners and flashing
  • High humidity ventilation to prevent mold and rot
  • UV-resistant underlayment for extreme heat cycles
  • Proper ice and water shield in valleys and penetrations

We have been roofing in this area for over a decade. We know what works here and what does not.

Roof Replacement FAQ

Common Questions

How much does a roof replacement cost in Wilmington?+
Costs vary based on roof size, material selected, pitch, and any underlying damage. A typical single-family home ranges from $8,000 to $25,000+. We provide free detailed estimates so you know exactly what to expect before any work begins.
How long does a roof replacement take?+
Most residential roof replacements are completed in 1-3 days. Larger homes, complex rooflines, or metal roofing installations may take slightly longer. We work rain or shine when safe to do so and always communicate timelines clearly.
Do you help with insurance claims?+
Yes. We document all storm damage with photos and detailed reports. We work directly with your insurance adjuster and provide everything needed to support your claim. Many of our customers have their roof fully covered by insurance after storm events.
What warranty do you offer?+
Our workmanship warranty covers installation quality. Additionally, manufacturer warranties apply to materials. GAF Timberline HDZ shingles come with a limited lifetime warranty. Metal roofing systems carry 30-50 year warranties depending on the product selected.
Can I stay home during the replacement?+
Yes. You can stay home during the entire process. There will be noise and vibration during tear-off and installation. We protect your landscaping, vehicles, and property throughout the job and do a thorough cleanup before we leave each day.
