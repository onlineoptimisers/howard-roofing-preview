Kitchen Remodeling FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a kitchen remodel take?+
Most full kitchen remodels take 4-8 weeks depending on scope, materials, and any structural changes. We provide a detailed timeline before work begins so you can plan accordingly.
How much does a kitchen remodel cost?+
Costs range from $15,000 for a basic refresh to $50,000+ for a full gut renovation. We work with your budget and provide transparent pricing with no hidden fees.
Do I need to move out during the remodel?+
No. Most homeowners stay in their home during the remodel. We set up dust barriers, protect your floors, and clean up daily. You may need to set up a temporary kitchen space with a microwave and cooler.
Can you work with my existing layout?+
Yes. We can update finishes, cabinets, and surfaces within your existing footprint, or we can reconfigure the entire layout including moving plumbing and electrical.
Do you handle permits?+
Yes. We pull all necessary permits and schedule inspections as required by New Hanover County. Everything is done to code.
