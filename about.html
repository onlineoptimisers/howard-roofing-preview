Our Story

A Father-Son Team Built on Trust

Howard Roofing & Construction was founded in 2016 by father-and-son team Scott and Chris Howard. Scott is a U.S. Marine Corps veteran with over 40 years of construction experience. Chris is an award-winning contractor with over 25 years in the industry whose passion for craftsmanship began on job sites alongside his dad and grandfather as a kid.

Together, they have built one of the most trusted roofing and remodeling companies in Wilmington and Coastal NC. Their approach is simple: do honest work, use quality materials, and treat every home like their own.
