What We Do Best

Six core services delivered with veteran discipline and over a decade of hands-on experience.

Roof Replacement

Full tear-off and new roof installation for residential and commercial properties. Shingle, metal, and flat systems built for North Carolina coastal conditions.

Metal Roofing

Standing seam and screw-down metal panels. Superior durability, energy efficiency, and 50+ year lifespan. Ideal for coastal properties.

Shingle Roofing

GAF Timberline HDZ architectural shingles with Class 4 impact resistance and 130 MPH wind warranty. The most popular choice in Wilmington.

Emergency tarping, full damage assessment, and insurance claim support. We document everything and work directly with your adjuster.

Flat Roofing

EPDM rubber and Elasco Flex systems for low-slope roofs. Seamless, waterproof, and backed by manufacturer warranties.
