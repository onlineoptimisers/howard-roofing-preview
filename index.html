Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a roof replacement take?+
Most residential roof replacements take 1-3 days depending on the size of your home, weather conditions, and the roofing material you select. We work efficiently while maintaining the highest quality standards, and we always clean up before we leave.
Do you offer free roof inspections?+
Yes. We offer complimentary roof inspections for homeowners in the Wilmington area. Our thorough inspection covers shingles, flashing, ventilation, gutters, and structural integrity. We will give you an honest assessment with no obligation.
What roofing materials do you work with?+
We install GAF Timberline HDZ asphalt shingles, standing seam metal roofing, screw-down metal panels, flat roofing systems (EPDM and Elasco Flex), and roof coatings. We will help you choose the best material for your budget and coastal conditions.
Do you help with insurance claims for storm damage?+
Yes. We work directly with your insurance company to document storm damage, provide detailed estimates, and ensure your claim covers the full scope of necessary repairs or replacement. We handle the paperwork so you can focus on your family.
What areas do you serve?+
We serve Wilmington, Hampstead, Rocky Point, Leland, Burgaw, Castle Hayne, Southport, Porters Neck, Kings Grant, Myrtle Grove, and surrounding communities in Eastern North Carolina.
Are you licensed and insured?+
Yes. Howard Roofing & Construction is fully licensed and insured. We carry general liability and workers compensation coverage for your protection. We are happy to provide proof of insurance upon request.
